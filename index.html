The Stars Program

How It Works

From your kitchen table to a child's hospital room — here's how a simple card becomes something unforgettable.

1

Cards Are Made

Volunteers create heartfelt handmade cards at our community craft events or from home — full of color, warmth, and encouraging words.

2

Gifts Are Gathered

Through donations from our community, we curate age-appropriate, hospital-safe gift items that bring smiles and comfort to kids of all ages.

3

We Deliver

Our team coordinates with Dallas-area children's hospitals to deliver cards and gifts directly to patients who need a little extra sunshine.

4

A Child Smiles

A sick child opens a card made just for them and a gift chosen with love — a small moment that means the whole world.

The Process

How Sprinkles Works

A simple, compliant process that gets good food to people in need — fast.

1

Restaurants Sign Up

Dallas-area restaurants register with us and let us know when they have surplus food that meets health and safety regulations.

2

We Coordinate

Our team quickly connects the restaurant with a nearby partner shelter or organization based on capacity and need.

3

Food Is Delivered

Food is picked up and delivered safely to the shelter — often the same day — reaching people who need it most.

4

Nobody Goes Hungry

Good food that would have gone to waste instead nourishes families, individuals, and communities across Dallas.

Good Food Shouldn't Go to Waste 🌿

Whether you run a restaurant or a shelter, there's a place for you in the Sprinkles network. Let's solve hunger together.
