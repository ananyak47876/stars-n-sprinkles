Stars 'n' Sprinkles

Turning community resources into hope and comfort across the Dallas area — one card, one gift, and one rescued meal at a time.

Our Mission

Stars 'n' Sprinkles transforms community resources into hope and comfort by delivering creative cheer to hospitalized children and safely rescuing surplus food to nourish individuals and families in need.

What We Do

Two branches, one purpose

✦

A two-branch nonprofit

One registered nonprofit project built around two missions — which is exactly why we're Stars 'n' Sprinkles.

★

The Stars branch

Gifting children in children's hospitals joy through handmade cards and thoughtful gifts.

●

The Sprinkles branch

Connecting restaurants with surplus food to homeless shelters, food banks, women's shelters, and people in need — cutting food waste across the Dallas area.
